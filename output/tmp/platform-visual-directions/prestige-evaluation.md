## Trade-offs

- Prestige 1 is the strongest scalable brand foundation and the best typographic system. Its quieter product relationship means later pages would need a more explicit application visual grammar.
- Prestige 2 is the most Swooshz-specific concept and best explains account -> workspace -> application through material form. Its sticky scene and pointer-responsive instrument require more careful production engineering.
- Prestige 3 creates the most memorable opening and the clearest Swooshz Quote Auto Generator theatre. It carries the highest implementation cost and needs disciplined motion budgets on content-heavy pages.

No winner is selected automatically. Prestige 1 is the best broad platform system, Prestige 2 is the strongest ownable Swooshz world, and Prestige 3 is the strongest launch-level product statement.
